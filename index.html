CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY (CSR)

Our CSR is geared towards human capacity development, helping the less-privileged in the society, visiting orphanage homes, organizing educational scheme for them, providing social welfare materials and foods for them according to Abraham’s Maslow hierarchy of needs.

PHGT Reality TV Show Platform is created for the general public, both for the able and dis-able human beings. Everyone is gifted with a talent. But the need for an enabling environment cannot be over-emphasized. The society is underdeveloped due to lack of this factor.

Building social structures, therefore, this is a clarion call to all well-meaning individual or organization to aid in facilitating projects that will train the less-privileged in the society.
