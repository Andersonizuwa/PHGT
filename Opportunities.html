TALENT IS NOT ENOUGH

A Talent is never enough, I communicate that while talent is important, there are millions of talented people who have ability but never get to use it and never reach their potential because they lack the characteristics that separate successful people from unsuccessful people. Those who neglect to make right choices to release and maximize their talent continually under-perform. These choices may be as simple as being punctual, giving effort, showing patience, or being unselfish. None of these choices requires talent but they sure enhances talent. Below is a list of those choices and characteristics that enhances a person’s talent and advances them in life.

Tips on how to build your Talents

Your belief will enable you to use your God given talent and showcase it to the world, believe in yourself.

Passion will enable you to a point of decision, working tirelessly with focus on your dreams.

When you take initiative in your talent, you will activate the development processes, thus bringing out the best in you.

The power of focus is key to the development of your talent; it will enable you to bring out the abilities to succeed with your talent.

Preparation + opportunity = success. Preparation takes a good time to achieve your dreams, you can’t jump at it. You must keep working with passion and focus, never giving up due to challenges.

Practice with evaluation makes perfect, you must accept responsibility to work and keep practicing on your talent.

Perseverance is a panacea for greatness, you must persevere through hardship.

You must be courageous in the face of challenges, trust your plan to achieve your dreams.

You must be teachable to enable you take your talent to the next level as you keep pushing towards your dreams.

the greatest threat to your talent is character, you must learn and develop a protective character to sustain your talent at the top.

All relationship influence our talent one way or the or the other discover whether your relationships are taking you in the best direction.

Accept responsibility to match the size of your talent, be responsive to the development process of your talent.

individuals make it to the top only with the help of others, learn how teamwork can work for you.

“Make these choices and you can become a talent-plus person. If you have talent. You stand alone. If you have talent plus, you stand out” (John C. Maxwell). “Talent for talent’s sake is a bauble and a show. Talent working with joy is the cause of universal truth lifts the possessor to a new power as a benefactor” (Raiph Waldo Emerson) Talent stands out, it gets you noticed. In the beginning, talent separates you from the rest of the pack. It gives you a head start on others. For that reason, natural talent is one of life’s greatest gifts. “The toughest thing about success is that you’ve got to keep on being a success. Talent is only a starting point in business. You’ve got to keep working that talent” (Irving Berlin) Too many talented people who start with an advantage over others lose that advantage because they rest on their talent instead of raising it.
